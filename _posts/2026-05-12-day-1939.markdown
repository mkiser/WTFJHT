2/ **U.S. inflation rose to 3.8% in April** – the highest level in nearly three years. Consumer prices rose 0.6% from March, with energy accounting for more than 40% of the monthly increase, with gasoline prices up 5.4%. Core inflation rose 2.8% from a year earlier, while real average hourly wages fell 0.5% from March – meaning prices rose faster than pay. ([NPR](https://www.npr.org/2026/05/12/nx-s1-5818279/inflation-cpi-gas-prices-housing-fuel-iran-war) / [New York Times](https://www.nytimes.com/2026/05/12/business/economy/cpi-inflation-report-consumer-prices.html) / [Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/us-consumer-prices-increase-further-april-2026-05-12/) / [NBC News](https://www.nbcnews.com/business/economy/april-inflation-data-iran-war-rcna344586) / [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/05/12/cpi-inflation-april-2026-.html) / [Associated Press](https://apnews.com/article/us-inflation-consumer-iran-war-3f11b7fdd20ea56d2f0895e5241af7b6) / [Washington Post](https://www.washingtonpost.com/business/2026/05/12/iran-inflation-trump-oil-gas-prices/))

3/ **The Congressional Budget Office estimated that Trump’s Golden Dome missile defense system could cost $1.2 trillion over 20 years** – nearly seven times more than Trump’s $175 billion estimate. Congress has approved about $24 billion for the program. The system would add ground- and space-based interceptors, sensors, and orbiting weapons, including a space-based interceptor layer of 7,800 satellites. The CBO said the system would still be overwhelmed by a full-scale attack from Russia or China. ([Associated Press](https://apnews.com/article/trump-golden-dome-price-tag-cbo-4f18f4ae8f1ba232abc097277ad0367a) / [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-05-12/price-tag-of-trump-s-golden-dome-shield-jumps-above-1-trillion) / [ABC News](https://abcnews.com/US/wireStory/trumps-proposed-golden-dome-estimated-cost-12-trillion-132890920) / [Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/aerospace-defense/us-budget-watchdog-estimates-golden-dome-will-cost-12-trillion-well-above-2026-05-12/))
