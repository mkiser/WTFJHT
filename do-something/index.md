<h1 class="page-title">Do Something.</h1>

<p class="lead">A normal person's guide to civic action.</p>

---

<div class="ds-reference">
<div class="ds-reference-body">

<details open name="start-here">
<summary id="voter-registration">Check or update your voter registration</summary>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://vote.gov/register">Vote.gov</a> — The federal government's voter registration site. Start here if you're not sure where to go.</li>
<li><a href="https://www.vote411.org/register">VOTE411</a> — Run by the League of Women Voters. Covers registration, what's on your ballot, and where to vote.</li>
<li><a href="https://www.vote.org/">Vote.org</a> — Register, check your status, request an absentee ballot, or set up election reminders.</li>
<li><a href="https://turbovote.org/">TurboVote</a> — Register once and get automatic reminders before every election. Email and text.</li>
<li><a href="https://www.rockthevote.org/">Rock the Vote</a> — Another solid registration tool, especially if you're registering for the first time.</li>
</ul>
</details>

<details name="start-here">
<summary id="overseas-military-accessibility">Voting from overseas, in the military, or with accessibility needs</summary>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://vote.gov/">Vote.gov</a> — Has specific guides for military families, voters living abroad, voters with disabilities, and new citizens.</li>
<li><a href="https://www.votefromabroad.org/">Vote From Abroad</a> — Request an absentee ballot and get help voting from outside the U.S.</li>
</ul>
</details>

<details name="start-here">
<summary id="find-your-reps">Find your representatives</summary>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://www.usa.gov/elected-officials">USA.gov: Elected Officials</a> — Type in your address and find out who represents you at every level.</li>
<li><a href="https://www.house.gov/representatives/find-your-representative">U.S. House: Find Your Representative</a> — Look up your House rep by ZIP code.</li>
<li><a href="https://www.senate.gov/senators/senators-contact.htm">U.S. Senate: Contact Your Senators</a> — Phone numbers, office addresses, and contact forms for your senators.</li>
<li><a href="https://openstates.org/find_your_legislator/">Open States</a> — Find your state legislators. A lot of the most consequential policy happens at the state and local level.</li>
<li><a href="https://ballotpedia.org/">Ballotpedia</a> — The Wikipedia of American politics. Covers everything from Congress to your school board.</li>
</ul>
</details>

<details name="start-here">
<summary id="voting-help">Voting help and election offices</summary>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://www.usa.gov/state-election-office">USA.gov: State and Local Election Offices</a> — Find your local election office. Useful for questions about polling places, absentee ballots, or registration issues.</li>
<li><a href="https://866ourvote.org/">Election Protection / 866-OUR-VOTE</a> — Call 866-OUR-VOTE if you run into problems voting. Free, nonpartisan, and they've been doing this since 2001.</li>
<li><a href="https://866ourvote.org/hotline/spanish/">Election Protection en Espanol</a> — Same hotline, in Spanish.</li>
<li><a href="https://www.usa.gov/state-local-elections">USA.gov: State and Local Elections</a> — See what elections are coming up where you live.</li>
</ul>
</details>

<details name="start-here">
<summary id="your-ballot">See what's on your ballot</summary>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://www.vote411.org/ballot">VOTE411 Ballot Guide</a> — Enter your address and see exactly what's on your ballot, with candidate info.</li>
<li><a href="https://www.vote411.org/plan-your-vote">VOTE411 Plan Your Vote</a> — Find your polling place, check your ballot, and make a plan before election day.</li>
</ul>
</details>

<details name="start-here">
<summary id="track-your-reps">Track what your representatives actually do</summary>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://www.govtrack.us/">GovTrack</a> — See what your members of Congress are actually doing: bills introduced, votes cast, attendance.</li>
<li><a href="https://www.congress.gov/">Congress.gov</a> — The official record. Read the actual text of bills, committee reports, and floor proceedings.</li>
<li><a href="https://openstates.org/">Open States</a> — Same idea, but for state legislatures. All 50 states.</li>
</ul>
</details>

</div>
</div>

<div class="ds-action" id="do-one-thing">
<h3><em>Do one thing today.</em></h3>
<p class="ds-action-sub">Got five minutes?</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://5calls.org/">5 Calls</a> — Get your reps, phone numbers, and a script. Then make the call.</li>
<li><a href="https://resist.bot/">Resistbot</a> — Text RESIST to 50409 to send letters to your elected officials by email, fax, or postal mail. From your mayor to the president.</li>
<li><a href="https://www.mobilize.us/">Mobilize</a> — Events near you: phone banks, canvasses, town halls, and volunteer shifts.</li>
<li><a href="https://indivisible.org/get-involved/take-action/">Indivisible Take Action</a> — They pick one thing and tell you what to do.</li>
<li><a href="https://indivisible.org/get-involved/campaigns/">Indivisible Campaigns</a> — Learn, act, win.</li>
<li><a href="https://susanrogan.substack.com/">Rogan's List</a> — Susan Rogan's running list of calls and actions.</li>
<li><a href="https://chopwoodcarrywaterdailyactions.substack.com/">Chop Wood, Carry Water</a> — Jess Craven's weekday newsletter. Short, specific, weekdays only.</li>
</ul>
</div>

### *Beyond the basics.*

If you want to go further.

<div class="ds-depth">

<div class="ds-depth-group">
<h4>Protect voting rights</h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://www.commoncause.org/take-action/">Common Cause</a> — Works on gerrymandering, dark money, and voter suppression.</li>
<li><a href="https://www.vote411.org/">League of Women Voters / VOTE411</a> — Nonpartisan. Around since 1920. Voter guides, candidate info, and election tools.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="ds-depth-group">
<h4>Defend civil liberties</h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://www.aclu.org/action">ACLU Action</a> — Current campaigns you can join. They'll tell you what to do and who to call.</li>
<li><a href="https://www.aclu.org/action/people-power">ACLU People Power</a> — The ACLU's volunteer network. Local events and trainings.</li>
<li><a href="https://www.aclu.org/know-your-rights">ACLU Know Your Rights</a> — What you can and can't do at a protest, at the border, in school, and when a cop pulls you over.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="ds-depth-group">
<h4>Join local organizing</h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://indivisible.org/get-involved/">Indivisible</a> — Local groups that actually meet and do things.</li>
<li>Your city council and school board meet regularly and most are open to the public. Look up your city or county website for when.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="ds-depth-group">
<h4>Volunteer from home</h4>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://votefwd.org/">Vote Forward</a> — Write handwritten letters to voters from your kitchen table. <a href="https://votefwd.org/instructions">Here's how it works.</a></li>
<li><a href="https://www.fieldteam6.org/">Field Team 6</a> — Help register voters. You can do it <a href="https://www.fieldteam6.org/all-volunteer-ops">from home</a> or <a href="https://www.fieldteam6.org/voter-drive-in-a-box">run a drive</a> in your neighborhood.</li>
</ul>
</div>

(function() {
  function openFromHash() {
    var hash = window.location.hash;
    if (!hash) return;
    var el = document.getElementById(hash.slice(1));
    if (el && el.tagName === 'SUMMARY') {
      document.querySelectorAll('.ds-reference-body details[open]').forEach(function(d) {
        d.removeAttribute('open');
      });
      var d = el.closest('details');
      if (d) {
        d.open = true;
        el.scrollIntoView({ block: 'start' });
      }
    }
  }

  openFromHash();
  window.addEventListener('hashchange', openFromHash);

  document.querySelectorAll('.ds-reference-body summary[id]').forEach(function(s) {
    s.addEventListener('click', function() {
      requestAnimationFrame(function() {
        var d = s.closest('details');
        if (!d) return;
        if (d.open) {
          history.replaceState(null, '', '#' + s.id);
        } else if (window.location.hash === '#' + s.id) {
          history.replaceState(null, '', window.location.pathname);
